SVSC Hosts Gul RS300 Grand Prix

by SVSC - Apr 10th, 2012 -

Sheffield Viking are proud to step in and host the 1st Gul RS300 Grand Prix for 2012 (previously scheduled for Draycote WSC). 

First scheduled starts

  • Saturday 21st April 1200hrs
  • Sunday 22nd April 1200hrs

3 races scheduled each day. Race briefing 1130hrs Saturday.

The racing on Sunday will be with club racing, with RS300s having own start.
Entry fee approx. £17/boat.

Directions to the club: google maps

For more details, see the association page: http://www.rs-association.com/index.asp?selection=Event&uid=494&fleet=RS300&detailevent=1

Novice Racing & Free Sailing

by SVSC - Aug 3rd, 2011 -

Club members are again invited to come and sail on 6th August at 1pm.

These sessions are aimed at any member new or old, whether boat owner or not, to come and try your sailing skills in a non-competitive environment. We will provide safety and organise short fun races if required.Come and test that new boat out, sail one of the club boats or crew for somebody else. 

For more information or to let me know you can come or if you are interested but can’t come this month contact Ava at novice-sailing@sheffieldviking.org.uk
